Our Story

Boost Subserve Engineering, also known as PRATIK Electrical Services, focuses on providing high-quality 400 KV electrical substation design, building, and maintenance services. With years of experience and a dedicated team of experts, they offer turnkey solutions that adhere to sustainability, dependability, and quality. BOOST SUBSERVE ENGINEERING is committed to innovation and constant improvement, ensuring their approaches are flexible enough to grow with the demands of the times. They aim to establish enduring relationships with clients based on respect, honesty, and trust. They also anticipate future obstacles and ensure their approaches are flexible enough to grow with the demands of the times.
